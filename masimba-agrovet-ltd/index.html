About Our Store

Masimba Agrovet Ltd is a premier pet store and agrochemicals supplier located in the heart of Nairobi, Kenya. We've been serving the community with quality products for pets and farm animals since our establishment.

Conveniently located opposite Mama Villa after Masimba Bus Stage on Kayole Spine Road, we're your one-stop shop for all pet needs and agricultural supplies.
