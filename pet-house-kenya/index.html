About Pet House Kenya

Your Trusted Pet Store in Kilimani

Located in the heart of Nairobi at Tropical Towers, Suite CF, Ground Floor Komo Lane, off Wood Ave, Pet House Kenya has been serving pet lovers since our establishment.

We pride ourselves on offering high-quality pet products, from premium pet food to stylish accessories and essential healthcare items. Our knowledgeable staff are passionate about pets and ready to help you find exactly what your furry, feathered, or scaled friends need.
