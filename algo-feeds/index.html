About Algo Feeds

Located in the heart of Mangu, Kenya, Algo Feeds has been serving the community with premium quality animal feeds since our establishment. We take pride in providing nutritionally balanced feeds for all types of livestock and pets.

Our products are carefully formulated by animal nutrition experts to ensure optimal growth, health, and productivity of your animals. We source only the highest quality ingredients and maintain strict quality control measures.
