About Our Agrovet

Menengai Agrovet & Veterinary Services is a trusted provider of animal feed and veterinary care in Nakuru, Kenya. Located conveniently on Kenyatta Avenue, we've been serving the local farming and pet community with quality products and professional services.

Our team of experienced veterinarians and agricultural specialists are dedicated to ensuring the health and well-being of your animals. Whether you're a small-scale farmer or a pet owner, we have the expertise and products to meet your needs.
