About Us

Menengai Agrovet & Veterinary Services has been serving the Nakuru community with quality animal feed and veterinary care for years. Located conveniently on Kenyatta Avenue, we pride ourselves on offering top-notch products and services for all your agricultural and pet needs.

Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to assist you with expert advice on animal nutrition, health, and general care. Whether you're a small-scale farmer or a pet owner, we have the solutions you need.

Our Services

Animal Feed

We offer a wide range of high-quality animal feeds for livestock, poultry, and pets. Our products are carefully selected to ensure optimal nutrition for your animals.

Veterinary Services

Our qualified veterinarians provide comprehensive health care services including vaccinations, deworming, treatment of illnesses, and general check-ups.

Pet Accessories

We stock a variety of pet accessories including collars, leashes, feeding bowls, grooming tools, and comfortable bedding for your beloved pets.

Agricultural Supplies

Beyond animal care, we provide essential agricultural supplies including seeds, fertilizers, and farming tools to support your farming activities.

Expert Advice

Our team offers professional advice on animal husbandry, nutrition, disease prevention, and general care to help you maintain healthy animals.

Quick Visits

Need something in a hurry? We offer quick visit services for customers who need immediate assistance or supplies without delay.
