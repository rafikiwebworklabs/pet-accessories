About Alpha Pet Pork

Located in the heart of Ruiru, Kenya, Alpha Pet Pork has been serving pet owners with premium pet supplies since our founding. We're passionate about providing high-quality products that enhance the lives of pets and their owners.

Our knowledgeable staff are pet lovers themselves, ready to help you find exactly what you need for your furry, feathered, or scaled family members. From nutritious food to fun toys and essential accessories, we've got you covered.
