About Our Reptile Store

Located in the beautiful Diani Beach area of Kenya, the African Snake Park Reptile Store is your premier destination for all things reptilian. We're passionate about educating the public about these fascinating creatures while providing quality pet accessories and supplies.

Our store is part of the Tropicana Shopping Centre in Ukunda, making us easily accessible to both locals and tourists exploring the Diani Beach Road area.
