About PETWORLD KENYA

Located in the heart of Nairobi at New Muthaiga Mall, PETWORLD KENYA has been serving pet lovers since our establishment. We pride ourselves on offering comprehensive pet care solutions under one roof.

Our team of passionate pet experts is dedicated to providing the highest quality products and services for all your furry, feathery, and scaly friends.

Whether you're looking for premium pet food, grooming services, or a safe place for your pet to stay while you're away, we've got you covered.
