About Our Hardware Store

Eagle Hardware Distributors Ltd is a premier hardware store located in Pioneer, Eldoret, Kenya. We've been serving the community with top-quality hardware products and exceptional service since our establishment.

Our store is conveniently located in the heart of Eldoret, making it easy for contractors, builders, and homeowners to access all their hardware needs in one place.

With a customer rating of 4.3 stars, we pride ourselves on our knowledgeable staff, competitive prices, and extensive inventory that caters to all your construction and home improvement needs.
